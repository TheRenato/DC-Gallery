This Discord bot will create a gallery of the images from one of your channels. You can add multiple channels, but every channel will get it's own url. The bot will detect when you add a new image in your channel and ad it to the gallery.

The images will not be hosted by us. We are just presenting them as a gallery. When the server restart your gallery will start from zero and you must do the build command again. This is to prevent unused gallery in the database and dead links.

We will add more functions to the DC-Gallery bot. As you could delete the gallery by your self. If you have ideas or trouble with the bot just put an issue in github.

Add Server Your Server

2

Write this in the channel you will add to the gallery.

!gallery build

To get your url to the gallery you just write:
